Support Center

Find answers to your frequently asked questions below:

1. How do I create an account on Nexus?

To create an account, access the Login page and follow the registration instructions.

2. How do I buy an NFT?

Once you've logged in, navigate to the Explore section, choose the NFT you want, and follow the purchase steps.

3. What payment methods are accepted?

We currently accept payments via bank card and Ethereum-compatible digital wallets.

4. How can I contact support?

You can contact our support team using the Contact page.

Steps to Become Verified

  1. Register and confirm your account using a valid email address.
  2. Complete your profile: upload a profile image, set your display name and bio.
  3. Identity verification: upload a government-issued ID (JPEG/PNG) and a selfie holding the ID (details must be visible).
  4. Tell us about you: write a short description explaining why you wish to create and sell NFTs.
